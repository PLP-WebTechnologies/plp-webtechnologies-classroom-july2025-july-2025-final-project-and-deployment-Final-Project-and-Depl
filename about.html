About Me

Hello! I'm Marvellous, a web developer based in Lagos, Nigeria. My journey into web development started with a simple "Hello World" and has grown into a full-blown passion for building dynamic, accessible, and responsive websites.

I specialize in frontend technologies but am also comfortable working on the backend. My goal is to leverage my skills to build high-quality web applications that solve real-world problems and provide exceptional user experiences.
