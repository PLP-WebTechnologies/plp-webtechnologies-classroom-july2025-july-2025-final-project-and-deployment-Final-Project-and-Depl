About M‑PesaAssist

M‑PesaAssist is an AI‑powered chatbot built to help M‑Pesa users with customer support and financial guidance. It aims to simplify your experience, answer FAQs, and aid in better money management.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to make mobile money safer, more understandable, and accessible. We envision a future where users can interact with support instantly and make informed financial decisions.

How It Works

  1. User sends a query (e.g., “How do I withdraw?” or “How much did I spend last week?”)
  2. Chatbot processes request via natural language processing and user data (with permission)
  3. Chatbot formulates a helpful response or guides to further action
  4. Optionally, hand off to human support if needed
