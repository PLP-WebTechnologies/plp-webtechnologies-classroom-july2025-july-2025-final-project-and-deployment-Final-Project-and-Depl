About EcoCharge

Pioneering the future of electric vehicle charging

Our Mission

At EcoCharge, we believe that transitioning to electric vehicles should be seamless and accessible for everyone. We're eliminating the barriers to EV adoption by making charging as simple as parking your car.

Founded in 2022 by EV enthusiasts and technology innovators, we've developed a wireless charging solution that works with existing infrastructure, making it perfect for urban environments where traditional charging stations are impractical.
